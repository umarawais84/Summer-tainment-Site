About Aruba

Aruba is a nice island in the Carribean with warm weather and nice beaches. It has 2 main beaches, Palm Beach, and Eagle Beach. The island is very small, spanning only 20 miles long and 6 wide.

What I Did

I was in an all inclusive resort and just chilled there the whole time. I went jet skiing and we tried a lot of different food throughout my stay.

Travel Tips

  • Rent a a scooter and bike to get around
  • Try some seafood
  • Visit both Eagle Beach and Palm Beach
